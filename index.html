EstateX Platform Overview

Why EstateX Exists

Property value compounds when capital, service fulfillment, and operations run as one system.

Finance needs to unlock the project

The sponsor needs capital to renovate the building, furnish the apartments, install smart devices, and prepare every unit for short-stay operation. Without a clean funding layer, the project never reaches execution.

Procurement needs to become fulfillment

Once funded, the property still needs furniture packages, smart locks, cleaning support, move-in kits, and staging. EstateX connects funding to real service delivery instead of leaving setup fragmented across disconnected vendors.

Operations need to create repeatable income

The real test starts after launch. Bookings, deposits, check-in rules, service requests, and payout distribution must stay visible and programmable so the asset can produce recurring operational value.

Illustrative Use Case: SkyNest

SkyNest demonstrates how the EstateX solution operates from start to recurring revenue.

SkyNest is an illustrative serviced-apartment conversion project used to show how PropFinX, HomeChain Store, and StayLedger work together as one integrated solution.

EstateX is not only used to fund the project, and not only used to manage bookings. It is used across the full lifecycle of the property.

01

Capital Entry

PropFinX raises the capital that makes SkyNest possible.

The sponsor submits the conversion project and financing request to PropFinX. After due diligence, selected financing rights are represented digitally through tokens, and investors complete wallet registration, KYC, AML, and whitelist approval before joining the offering.

Smart contracts issue the tokens, record ownership, and apply compliance rules. In practice, this is the stage that funds renovation, furnishing, smart-device installation, and every other downstream step in the SkyNest rollout.

02

Asset Preparation

HomeChain Store turns funded capital into a usable, service-ready property.

After financing closes, the sponsor uses HomeChain Store to buy furniture packages, smart locks, cleaning, move-in kits, and staging support. Payments can use ecosystem tokens, stablecoins, or other supported methods.

Once merchants or providers complete the work, smart contracts can release automated payouts across the merchant, installer, cleaner, logistics provider, and platform operator. The case moves from raised capital to real-world execution.

03

Operational Monetization

StayLedger converts prepared apartments into recurring rental income.

Once the building is ready, the serviced apartments are listed on StayLedger so guests can browse, book, and pay. Smart contracts manage booking logic, deposits, cancellation rules, and check-in / check-out conditions.

During each stay, the platform can still call cleaning or maintenance through HomeChain Store. After completion, deposits and revenues are distributed automatically among the host, property manager, cleaner, and platform, closing the full operating loop.
