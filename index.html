Market Problem

Why Our Solution Is Built as a Closed Loop

Traditional property projects face high friction and low liquidity in financing, fragmented procurement and fulfillment in service delivery, and weak transparency in deposits, payouts, and operating data. Together, these constraints reduce asset efficiency and slow value realization.

Our platform is not designed as a token experiment. Our architecture places capital formation, service fulfillment, and operational monetization inside one verifiable rule set, where smart contracts turn cross-party coordination into executable workflows.

Our commercial thesis is simple: structure capital, activate the asset, and convert operations into durable cash flow.

Our Services

Three Services, One Institutional Execution Stack

PropFinX — Web3 Real Estate Finance

Our finance service tokenizes selected financing rights and embeds compliance checks, issuance logic, custody, and transfer constraints into smart contracts. It gives sponsors and investors a controlled, transparent framework for capital participation.

  • Project intake, due diligence, and compliance approval
  • Investor onboarding with KYC / AML / whitelist checks
  • Token issuance, ownership records, and transfer policies
  • Post-issuance monitoring and position-level reporting

HomeChain Store — Property Goods & Services

Our service marketplace unifies property procurement and fulfillment in one governed process. Funded assets can source furniture, equipment, cleaning, and maintenance through standardized pay-to-fulfill-to-settle workflows.

  • Vendor onboarding and standardized service bundles
  • EST / stablecoin payments with service redemption records
  • Automated multi-party settlement on fulfillment confirmation
  • Trackable quality, fulfillment, and supplier performance data

StayLedger — Rental & Accommodation Operations

Our operations platform codifies booking, deposits, cancellation logic, payout distribution, and service coordination. It moves assets from financeable to consistently monetized.

  • Standardized listing and booking operations
  • Deposit escrow and policy-based release mechanisms
  • Automated revenue distribution across all stakeholders
  • Operational data intelligence for long-term asset strategy
